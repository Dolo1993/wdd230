Our History

Fruit Drinks was founded in 1998 by John and Jane Doe, who were passionate about creating healthy and delicious drinks for their community. Over the years, we have expanded our menu to include a wide variety of fruit drinks, smoothies, and juices.

We are proud to be located in Carlsbad, a beautiful coastal city in Southern California known for its sunny weather, great surf, and amazing seafood.

Our mission is to provide our customers with the freshest and healthiest drinks possible, using only the highest quality ingredients and innovative recipes.
